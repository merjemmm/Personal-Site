Lake Erie Analysis in Python

Lake Erie is known for having Severe Harmful Algal Blooms in the summer. So, a team of us did analysis on available nutrient data for Lake Erie. We found there was correlation between water runoff and high nutrient levels in the water. Interestingly, 2020 had one of the smallest algal blooms in the past years. This could point to how much human actions impact the water quality.
